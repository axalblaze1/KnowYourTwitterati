Know your Twitterati!

Beware of fraud twitteratis who use Black marketing sites to gain followers. Buying fake followers is as easy as ordering a snack on Swiggy. They can spread fake news and incite voilence therefore it is important for you to know your Twitteraties. We use Neural Networks to analyze behaviour of accounts and give an confidence index for a user.

Personal Confidence Index

This represents the individual user's Confidence Index, by appling the neural network model on the given user. This is index should not be considered when the given user is a influcener/celebrity/leader or a person with high twitter activity. For such users use Cummulative Confidence Index.

Cummulative Confidence Index

This will fetch 20 most recent followers of the account and fetch their data and apply the neural network model on each follower and give the avg score. This index sholud be considered when the given user is a influcener/celebrity/leader or a person with high twitter activity.

About!

We are a team of Engineering Graduate students at JSS Academy of Technical Education of Noida, India Computer Science and Eng Department. Our mission is to help create a better, safer environment on the social media platform Twitter, by identifying black market customers buying fake followers and thus cutting down the fake informations and possibily malicious opinions.
This platform gives a confidence index for any user of twitter by analysing over 60 different attributes of the handle, using artificial intelligence model. 1000+ Twitter handles were analysed and used for model training with accuracy of 71.12 percent. Platform simply needs the username name, and it does rest of the work, making it user friendly and an easy approach for one to filter out their followers and followings, thus getting rid off spams. The platform gives a full account overview, not just about the authenticity but also analyses user’s activity throughout the week, generating graphs and pie chart, making it an interesting sneak peek.



What are blackmarketing Sites?

There are many sites online where people buy fake followers, likes, retweets or reposts for all social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.

How it affects you?

People with malicious intent can increase their reach inorganically using these sites and spread fake news or incite voilence. Therefore it is important to identify such people.

How we solve this?

We assign two indicies PCI and CCI to each user, which gives an estimate of the users's trust-worthyness. We use 60+ features to predict a user Index.
